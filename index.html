About Us


The vision of ‘The CORE’ is to promote disarmament in all its forms in various different levels. While the definition of this word ‘disarmament’ means the reduction or withdrawal of military forces and weapons disarmament is more than this. For this campaign disarmament also means reduction of unnecessary violence. For the members of this campaign, controlled violence is always the last option because this campaign also believes that everything has a limit and we believe in responding but not reacting to any grave situation.

CORE also promotes that disarmament which means disapproval against poaching or any other physical or mental harassment or harm. It also defames the use of non-conventional weapons. The idea of disarmament for this campaign stands on the four goals taken from SDGs which act like pillars to our agenda; those Goal no. 4, 8, 11 and 16. To conclude disarmament for the members of the Core is to promote peace everywhere and it also carries to the point that war it is never the immediate solution of any problem.
